❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you play on my account?

Nope! I only offer duo queue boosts. You play on your account, I play on mine — no risk of bans or login issues.

What if we lose games during the boost?

I aim for very high win rates. If we lose a game, I’ll simply play more to make sure we hit the goal. No extra cost.

Can I choose which hero or role you play?

Yes! You can request a hero or role you'd like me to use to complement your style. I'm flexible with most roles.

How fast can I expect to climb?

Depends on your current rank and order size. Most boosts are completed within 1–3 days with flexible scheduling.

Do you offer coaching?

Yes! I offer live coaching during the boost for an additional fee. You’ll learn strategy, laning, teamfighting, and macro.

How do I pay?

We’ll confirm payment after agreeing on the order. I currently accept PayPal, WeChat Pay, and other options upon request.
