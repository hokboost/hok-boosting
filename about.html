🧑‍💼 About Me

Hey, I’m a professional Honor of Kings booster with over 5 seasons of duo queue experience. I’ve helped players across multiple regions climb safely and quickly — all while playing their own accounts, without any account sharing.

I specialize in win-focused boosting with smart strategies — like using low-score partner accounts to match into easier games and speed up the climb. I adapt to any role and keep things smooth, chill, and consistent.
